HOW IT WORKS

FIND YOUR ARTIST

Explore the wide range of Entertainers and go through their profiles and testimonials. Click the 'Book' button on Artist's Profile and fill in your budget and other details about your Event. The Artist will get a notification and go through your requirements.

ARTIST'S CONFIRMATION

Now sit-back and wait for Artist's response. Once your budget and details suits the artist's requirement, He/She/They will accept the request and confirm the booking upon which you will get a notification on whether the artist accepted or rejected the offer.

SECURE PAYMENT

Once the artist accepts your booking request You can finally proceed for payment by using the secure PayTm Online Payment gateway. Now You can relax and wind up to enjoy your favourite entertainer's dashing performance on your event day!
